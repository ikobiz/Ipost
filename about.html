About Ipost

ipost is a minimalistic social feed app built with Node.js, Express, and a touch of nostalgia. It’s designed for clarity, modularity, and pixel-perfect control—no mobile-first fluff, no bloated layouts.

Every feature is handcrafted in 2 days, from login and sign-up to persistent post storage. It’s a celebration of simplicity, authenticity, and the joy of building things that just work.

This project is a personal experiment in UI/UX minimalism, robust backend design, and iterative development. If you're reading this, you're part of the journey.
